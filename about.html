A2 Exploration Cult

The A2 Exploration Cult is a student organization that provides the unique experience that combines exploring the Ann Arbor area and the University of Michigan campus, and a cult-like experience with special titles and more. Most of the images you'll see on this website are photos taken by members!

Titles as a Cult

The Great Leader believes in title inflation. Therefore, you may request any pretentious and unique title.
